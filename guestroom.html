COURTYARD STANDARD ROOMS



Opening into wide verandas with comfortable sit-outs, the Courtyard Standard rooms offer 300 square feet of character and old-world charm. The thoughtful conveniences provided leave nothing to be desired. With large windows leading to the sit-outs, comfortable beds to sink into at the end of the day and immaculate interiors, these rooms at Hotel Ratan Vilas Jodhpur promise a picturesque stay.

WELCOME TO THE Guest Rooms Area of Ratan Vilas



A stay in one of the cosily accoutred guestrooms at Ratan Vilas Jodhpur is so much more than just a night at a hotel- it’s a sampling of Rajasthan’s fabled hospitality. Each one of these guestrooms narrate the tale of the elegant mansion and its noble lineage through vintage furnishings interspersed with delicate Indian motifs and arresting sepia photographs. Enjoy a beverage in the courtyard on a cool evening, or sharpen your quill over an antique desk in your room- there are plenty of ways to soak in the tranquillity that is all - pervading.

POOL FACING SUPERIOR ROOMS



The grandest and most-sought after set of rooms in Ratan Vilas - these pool-facing chambers take you back to the august days of Rajput nobility. Each room opens out to an intimate veranda overlooking the pristine swimming pool through the lively garden. A sunken tub and soft bathrobes conclude the luxurious experience at one of the best heritage hotels in Jodhpur.
